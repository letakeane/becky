ABOUT

Becky Robison writes novels, short stories, and flash fiction.

The first book she ever wrote was a 100 page collection of stories called Ghost, Spooks, and Other Scary Things, written and illustrated in the second grade with her best friend.

Since then, Becky has continued to create. Becky holds an MFA in Creative Writing from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. Her work ranges from horror-tinged flash fiction to coming-of-age YA novels that meditate on identity, queerness, and beliefs.
